Galactus is an anonymous, privacy-based cryptocurrency. It presents itself as a branch of Monero by relying on the enhanced privacy features. Galactus also has features of its own. Thus, Galactus could initiate a new generation of anonymous currencies that would authorize the execution of decentralized and anonymous transactions.
